About Bakers.in

Bakers.in started as a single neighbourhood oven and grew into a one-stop kitchen for everything you crave — cakes for birthdays, pizza for movie nights, burgers for quick bites, and mocktails for when it's just too hot outside.

Every order is made fresh after you place it — nothing sits around waiting. We bake, grill, fry and shake to order, then get it to your door as fast as we can.
